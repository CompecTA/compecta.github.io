Deep Learning with CompecTA SuperNova X™ Series

Super-fast & Scalabe Deep Learning & AI Server Systems with up to 10-GPUs

Deep learning is the fastest-growing field in machine learning. It uses many-layered Deep Neural Networks (DNNs) to learn levels of representation and abstraction that make sense of data such as images, sound, and text.

All-In-One & Ready to Use

CompecTA SuperNova Engine X™ is a super-fast and efficient in single-root complex server solution for Deep Learning needs.

With up to 10GPUs in 4U form factor CompecTA SuperNova Engine X™ provides top deep learning & AI performance and parallelization.

It comes with Deep Learning Neural Network libraries, Deep Learning Frameworks, Operating System and Drivers pre-installed and ready to use.

Enterprise Level Support

CompecTA SuperNova Engine X™ comes with an Enterprise Level Support package from HPC experts with over 20 years of experience in the field.

We will assist you with any problem you may encounter with your system.


Free CPU Time

CompecTA SuperNova Engine X™ comes with 100 thousands hours of CPU Time on CompecTA's in-house HPC Cluster service called FeynmanGrid™.

Use this opportunity to gain experience on using HPC Clusters or performance test any application or to test some new code/application.
